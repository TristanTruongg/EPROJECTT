Tanjung Rhu beach

Tanjung Rhu is a 1,5 km long beach located on the north coast of Langkawi. The resort is uncrowded, solitary and calm.


Beach description

The coast is covered in grainy white sand, with casuarina trees growing nearby. The descent is smooth and the water is shallow. The panoramic view of the blue sea and the nearby islands is fascinating. Tourists come to Tanjung Rhu for relaxation and the amazing beauty.

Tanjung Rhu is visually divided into three zones. The first zone belongs to a fashionable hotel with all the necessary conveniences. The second zone will shock visitors with numerous restaurants and an ice rink. The third zone is the calmest one - a perfect place for relaxation. There are no cheap bungalows or guest houses on the beach. Underwater swimming with scuba gear is a popular activity on Tanjung Rhu.


When is the best time to go?

There are no sudden changes in temperature on the beaches of Malaysia's and possible to relax on the islands all year round. There is no particular season of rains and winds, precipitation is distributed depending on the month of the year and the region of the country.
