Lazy Beach

Lazy Beach is located on the coast of Koh Rong Samloem. The trip to the island by boat is 2 hours, but it's worth it.


Beach description

There is pure golden sand. Clear water is almost always calm. The beach is cleaned daily, so here you can safely walk on the sand without fear of injury. The beach is surrounded by pine trees, in the shadow of which monkeys jump. They do not react at all to vacationers, so they should not be afraid

Here tourists have only communication with nature and complete merger with it. Snorkeling in water is good. The middle part of the bottom is sand, but there are stones along the edges and at a shallow depth; various fish swim there. Scuba diving enthusiasts can organize diving on the island. Shore or sea fishing is offered to fishermen.


On the coast there are cozy bungalows of an unusual shape. Restaurants on the coast are also there but not as noisy as in Sihanoukville. It is generally not customary to make noise here, so Lazy Beach is best for couples.


When is the best time to go?

The Cambodia's climate is humid and hot. The rainiest months – are August and September. The air temperature throughout the year does not fall below + 23˚С. While April – is the hottest month. At this time, the air temperature is in the region of + 33- + 38 ° C. The best time for travelling to the coast of Cambodia is from November to February. Water at this time warms up to + 30˚С.
