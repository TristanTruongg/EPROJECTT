Otres beach

Otres beach is located on the southern outskirts, 8 km from the center of Sihanoukville. It is divided into three zones. In total, the beach stretches for several kilometers. Everywhere are calm water and pure white sand, when the current does not bring jellyfish, algae and other garbage. Coniferous trees and many bushes grow along the beach. The territory is green.


Beach description

In the first part of the beach (Otres 1), it is convenient to relax with children. There is a playground and other amenities. In the vicinity, there are many hotels and restaurants. You can ride catamarans, scooters, boats, go diving, snorkeling and fishing.

Further the coast narrows, and the beach becomes wilder. Along the coast, there are a lot of waste and algae. Sometimes clean sand is brought here from other beaches to improve this area of.


In the second part of Otres are more luxurious hotels. The beach strip is much wider. Sun beds and umbrellas are free. The place is suitable for families with children. The water is calm, and the sand is fine and clean. It is calm and comfortable here. But there are no cafes nearby where you can eat. There are not even shops to buy water.

In the third part of the beach, locals come here with family or youth companies and organize their barbecue and drink parties.

You can get to the beach for $ 5 by tuk-tuk motorbike.

When is the best time to go?

The Cambodia's climate is humid and hot. The rainiest months – are August and September. The air temperature throughout the year does not fall below + 23˚С. While April – is the hottest month. At this time, the air temperature is in the region of + 33- + 38 ° C. The best time for travelling to the coast of Cambodia is from November to February. Water at this time warms up to + 30˚С.
