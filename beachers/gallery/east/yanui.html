Yanui beach

Yanui Beach is located between the Windmill observation deck and the famous Promthep Cape in the very south of Phuket. Yanui is widely known among tourists, especially fans of snorkeling, diving and kayaking.


Beach description

A unique rock formation divides the Ya Nui Bay in two parts. In front of it there is an island surrounded by greenery. Along the edges of the beach area there slopes covered with forbs rise. The water in the lagoon surprises with emerald hues and transparency, except perhaps in the northern part, where a small river flows into the sea. At guests disposal there is the rent of beach equipment and a diving center. The thatched-roofed beachfront restaurant serves Thai dishes.

It is very easy to find Yanui. From Patong Beach, you should lean toward Nai Harn Beach. Taking (following the signs) a course to Promthep Cape and Rawai beach, you will reach the parking lot. Next to it there will be a beach restaurant, and on the contrary, Ya Nui himself.


When is the best time to go?

The best time for a beach holiday in Phuket lasts from November to April or May. The weather during this period is sunny, calm, the sea is calm, there are almost no waves. The height of the beach season falls on December, when a number of major international holidays are added to water activities. Prices for airline tickets, accommodation, services, food and transportation are at their maximum.
